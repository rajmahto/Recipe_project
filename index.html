Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe:

  1. Make Dough: Mix 1 cup milk powder, 1/4 cup flour,
    1/4 tsp baking soda,2 tbsp ghee, and 2-3 tbsp milk. Rest for 10 minutes.
  2. Shape Balls: Form smooth, crack-free balls from the dough.
  3. Fry Balls: Deep fry until golden brown. Drain excess oil.
  4. Make Syrup & Soak: Boil 1 cup sugar, 1 cup water, cardamom
    Add saffron (optional) and rose water. Soak fried balls in syrup for 30 minutes.

Kaju Barfii

Ingredients:

  • Sugar
  • Cashew nuts
  • Saffron threads
  • Ghee

Recipe:

  1. Grind Cashews: Grind 2 cups of cashews into a fine powder.
  2. Make Dough:Cook cashew powder with 1 cup sugar and 1/2 cup
    water until it forms a dough.Add 1 tbsp ghee and mix well..
  3. Roll & Flatten: Roll the dough between parchment paper until 1/4 inch thick.
  4. Cut & Cool: Cut into diamond shapes and let cool before serving.

Samosa

Ingredients:

  • Miada
  • Potatos
  • Green Chillis,Ginger,Cumin Seeds,Green peas
  • Oil
  • Water

Recipe

  1. Make Dough: Mix 2 cups flour, 1/4 cup oil, and water
    to form a stiff dough. Rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare Filling: Cook 2 cups mashed potatoes with peas,
    spices (cumin, coriander, garam masala), and salt.
  3. Shape & Fill: Roll dough into circles, cut in half,
    form cones, and fill with potato mixture.
  4. Fry Samosas: Deep fry in hot oil until golden
    brown. Drain excess oil and serve.

Pani Puri

Ingredients:

  • Semolina
  • Maida
  • Salt
  • water
  • Mint leaves,Coriander leaves
  • Green Chillis,Ginger
  • Oil
  • Chaat Masala

Recipe

  1. Prepare Puris:Buy or make puris by frying small, flat discs
    of semolina dough until puffed and crispy.
  2. Make Pani: Blend mint, coriander, green chilies,tamarind, and spices
    with water to make spicy, tangy water. Chill.
  3. Prepare Filling: Mix boiled potatoes, chickpeas, onions, and spices.
  4. Assemble & Serve: Poke a hole in each puri, fill with the potato
    mixture, and add spicy water. Serve immediately..

Masala Dosa

Ingredients:

  • Rice,Urad dal,Chana dal
  • Fenugreek seeds,Poha
  • Potatos,Onions,ginger,Green Chillis
  • Oil or Ghee

Recipe

  1. Prepare Batter: Soak 1 cup rice and 1/2 cup urad dal
    for 6 hours. Grind into a smooth batter, ferment overnight.
  2. Make Potato Filling: Cook mashed potatoes with onions,
    mustard seeds,turmeric, curry leaves, and green chilies.
  3. Cook Dosas: Spread a ladle of batter thinly on a hot,
    greased pan. Cook until crisp.
  4. Fill & Serve: Place potato filling in the center of the
    dosa, fold, and serve with chutney and sambar.

Biryani

Ingredients:

  • Rice,onions,tomatoes, yogurt
  • Water,Bay leaves,Cloves,Salt
  • Chiken,Yougurt,Ginger,Galric,Lemon
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Cook Rice: Parboil 2 cups of basmati rice
    with whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom).
  2. Prepare Meat/Veggies: Cook meat or vegetables with onions,
    tomatoes, yogurt, and biryani spices until tender.
  3. Layer: In a pot, layer the meat/veggies and rice, adding
    saffron milk, fried onions, and fresh herbs between layers.
  4. Dum Cook: Seal the pot with dough or foil,
    cook on low heat for 20-30 minutes. Serve hot..
